Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-22

This Privacy Policy explains how the Hasbro Black Series Checklist app handles information when you use the app.

Summary

The app is designed to work primarily on-device. It does not require you to create an account, and the developer does not operate a user database for your collection data.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Collection status, including whether an item is marked as collected.
  • Wishlist and favorites selections.
  • Quantity and opened or sealed status.
  • Notes you add to individual items.
  • Photos you add to your collection entries.
  • App preferences such as view mode and grouping preferences.
  • Backup files you choose to export.
  • Developer Mode settings, including any custom catalog URL you enter.
  • Local premium state information needed to remember entitlements on your device.

Photos And Camera Access

If you choose to use photo features, the app may request permission to access your camera or photo library. These permissions are optional and are only used for the collection photo features you choose to use.

Network Requests

The app may make network requests in the following cases:

  • To download the latest catalog JSON from the app’s public GitHub-hosted catalog source.
  • To load remote product images referenced by catalog entries.
  • To open official item links in your browser when you choose to visit a product page.
  • To communicate with Apple for In-App Purchase and restore purchase flows.
  • To load a custom catalog source if you enable Developer Mode and enter your own URL.

In-App Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple using StoreKit and the App Store. The developer does not receive your full payment card details. Apple may provide transaction and entitlement information needed to unlock paid features inside the app.

What The Developer Does Not Intentionally Collect

  • No required user account information.
  • No advertising identifiers for the developer’s own tracking.
  • No third-party analytics SDK operated by the developer.
  • No cloud account used by the developer to store your collection data.

Sharing

The developer does not intentionally sell your personal information. Data may be transmitted to third parties only when necessary for app functionality, such as Apple for purchases, GitHub-hosted catalog delivery, image hosts referenced by the catalog, or your browser when you open external links.

Data Retention And Deletion

Your collection data remains on your device until you delete it, reset the app’s data, remove the app, or overwrite it by restoring a backup. Exported backup files remain wherever you save them until you delete them.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children through developer-operated accounts or servers.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data handling change. The latest published version on this page should be treated as the current policy.

Support And Contact

Support requests can be submitted through the project repository: GitHub Issues.
